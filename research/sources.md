## Common Patterns Found

### Content Style
- Short paragraphs
- Strong opening hook
- Personal experiences
- Educational insights
- Actionable takeaways

### Engagement Drivers
- Storytelling
- Contrarian viewpoints
- Frameworks and templates
- Real business examples
- Audience-focused advice

### Posting Formats
- Text-only posts
- Carousel documents
- Screenshots
- Case studies
- Step-by-step frameworks

### Posting Goals
- Build authority
- Increase reach
- Generate inbound leads
- Create audience trust
- Drive community engagement

---

## Initial Research Insights

1. Educational content consistently performs well.
2. Personal stories increase engagement.
3. Strong hooks are critical for stopping the scroll.
4. Framework-based posts are frequently shared.
5. Consistency appears more important than virality.
6. Audience value is prioritized over direct selling.
7. Most successful creators focus on one core niche.
8. Clear positioning strengthens personal brand growth.
